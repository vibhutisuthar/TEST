Important Message from Virgin Blue  
Effective immediately, the Airline Record Locators provided to our Guests, when making bookings via the GDS, will no longer be recognised when using the Virgin Blue Self Check-In product suite.
Guests should use the following procedure when travelling with Virgin Blue -
(1) Web Check-In - Guests may use their Velocity Rewards Membership number to check in online, if not already a member please login at www.velocityrewards.com.au. It's free to join.
(2) Kiosk Check-In - Guests may swipe their Velocity Rewards Membership card or any personal credit card that is a correct name match to the travel booking.
The Airline Reference provided on this itinerary can only be used via the normal check-in procedure at the Airport Counters, along with photo identification.
